Frequently Asked Questions

Binghatti Circle offers luxury residential apartments that vary between studios, 1 bedroom, 2 bedroom, and 3 bedroom apartments. The project comprises approximately 776 residences with various layouts designed to cater to different preferences and family sizes.

Binghatti Circle is the tallest residential tower in JVC, consisting of 2 basement levels, 4 parking levels, 58 residential floors, 3 mechanical floors, and a roof.

The completion date for Binghatti Circle is Q2, 2027.

The standard payment plan structure is:
  • 20% Down Payment when signing the contract
  • 50% During construction
  • 30% Upon Completion

Binghatti Circle has an excellent investment potential due to its:
  • Tallest Tower Status: The tallest residential tower in JVC, which is a landmark itself, in addition to the premium views you will enjoy.
  • Strategic Location: The project lies in Jumeirah Village Circle (JVC) with easy access to Al Khail Road, Sheikh Mohammed Bin Zayed Road, and major Dubai attractions.
  • Flexible Payment Plan: The project offers a flexible payment plan for its customers, which is an opportunity you canâ€™t miss.

Binghatti Circle lies in Jumeirah Village Circle (JVC), with great connectivity to major roads, including Al Khail Road and Sheikh Mohammed Bin Zayed Road, in addition to being near many places like Circle Mall, Dubai Hills Mall, Palm Jumeirah, Dubai Media City, and many other attractions.
