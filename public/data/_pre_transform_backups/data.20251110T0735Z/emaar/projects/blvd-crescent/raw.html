A STYLISH NEW ADDITION TO DOWNTOWN DUBAI

BLVD Crescent in Downtown Dubai

Rising above Sheikh Mohammed bin Rashid Boulevard, BLVD Crescent comprises two residential towers of 39 and 21 storeys with 307 stylish apartments and a beautifully landscaped pool area. The stunning one, two and three-bedroom homes feature contemporary design, quality fixtures and large floor-to-ceiling windows that let in an abundance of light and frame breathtaking views of Downtown Dubai the perfect choice for contemporary Dubai living.

THERE'S LIFE ALL ALONG THE BOULEVARD

BLVD Crescent is ideally positioned alongside the buzzing Sheikh Mohammed bin Rashid Boulevard with its dozens of restaurants, cafs and scenic spots to enjoy. The two-tower complex further offers easy access to Burj Khalifa, Dubai Opera and The Dubai Mall for whenever you want to indulge in shopping, entertainment or an inspiring stroll. It is also minutes away from Business Bay and DIFC perfect for when business calls.

A FRESH PERSPECTIVE ON THE OUTSIDE WORLD

Depending on its position, your new home could boast views of the awe-inspiring Burj Khalifa and Dubai Opera, as well as the colourful Sheikh Mohammed bin Rashid Boulevard. The 4-bedroom limited-edition penthouses each with their own private terraces and balconies offer the most stunning views of all, stretching all the way to the Arabian Sea. Take a seat and witness the sunset above the city’s most iconic destination, every day.

DUBAI STYLE PERSONIFIED

And once you have had your fill of the view, step outside and explore Downtown Dubai’s trendy restaurants, swanky fashion outlets and all of your favourite attractions. BLVD Crescent simply offers you that iconic Dubai lifestyle, in a dream location.

Amenities

Both towers are uniquely designed to face onto a beautifully landscaped podium level that boasts two swimming pools, a childrens play area and shaded relaxation areas. Sports and wellness amenities include a state-of-the-art fitness centre, while the ground level provides convenient restaurant & retail selections for an everyday resort-style lifestyle.

THE ULTIMATE ADDRESS

Downtown Dubai, our flagship mega-development, aptly named The Centre of Now, spans two of the most prestigious square kilometres in the city. Positioned between Sheikh Zayed Road and Financial Centre Road, this dynamic hub has driven the economy and cemented Dubais reputation as a true global icon.

At the heart of the emirate, Downtown Dubai offers a metropolitan lifestyle surrounded by world-class landmarks, entertainment, and glamour. One of the key attractions in the area is Dubai Mall, the worlds largest shopping mall, offering an unrivalled selection of retail and dining options.

Properties in Downtown Dubai represent the pinnacle of luxury living, with a skyline dominated by contemporary high-rise residences, complemented by world-class amenities and a thriving cultural scene. From sleek condominiums with panoramic views of the iconic skyline to elegant townhouses nestled along tree-lined avenues, each home exudes sophistication and style.

With premium amenities such as private pools, state-of-the-art fitness centres, and exclusive concierge services, these properties elevate the concept of urban living to new heights. Whether seeking a cosmopolitan penthouse or a family-friendly retreat, Downtown Dubai offers an unmatched opportunity to experience the epitome of opulent city living.
